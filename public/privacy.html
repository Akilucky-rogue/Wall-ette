Wall·ette — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 June 2026 · Applies to the Wall-ette Android app and wall-e-7a113.web.app

Wall-ette is a personal finance tracker operated by an individual developer ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app handles and how. The short version: your financial data belongs to you, is stored in your own private account space, and is never sold, shared for advertising, or used for any purpose other than showing you your own finances.

Data we collect

  • Account data: your email address and a password, used solely to create and authenticate your account (via Google Firebase Authentication).
  • Financial data you provide: transactions you enter manually and transactions extracted from bank statements you choose to import (amounts, dates, merchant names, categories, notes, account opening balance).
  • App settings: preferred currency, daily spend limit, and similar preferences.
  • Security log: timestamps and method (password/biometric) of app unlock attempts, stored in your own account space so you can review access to your wallet.

We do not collect contacts, location, advertising identifiers, or analytics profiles, and the app contains no advertising.

How bank statement imports work

Statement files (PDF/Excel) are parsed entirely on your device. The file itself is never uploaded to any server. Only the resulting transaction records (date, amount, merchant, category) are saved to your private database space so they sync across your devices.

Where data is stored

  • Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication) stores your account and transaction data, encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is restricted by security rules so only your authenticated account can read or write your data. See Firebase Privacy & Security.
  • Your device keeps an offline copy (cache) so the app works without a connection. Uninstalling the app removes this local copy.

Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database hosting (data processor).
  • exchangerate-api.com — the app fetches public currency exchange rates. This request contains no personal data or financial information.
  • Google Fonts — the web version loads fonts from Google's CDN; standard web-request metadata (IP address) applies.

No data is sold or shared with advertisers, brokers, or any other third party.

Insights are computed locally

All analytics — spending insights, recurring-charge detection, income analysis — are computed on your device from your own data. Nothing is sent to any AI service or external analytics platform.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. You can delete all transactions from within the app (History → Clear All). To delete your account and all associated data permanently, email akshatbvora@gmail.com from your registered address and we will remove it within 30 days.

Your rights

You may export a full copy of your data at any time from the app's Data Studio (Export → Full Backup), and you may request access, correction, or deletion by email.

Children

Wall-ette is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
